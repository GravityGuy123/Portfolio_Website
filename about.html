About Me

Hello! I'm Simon Ejidike, a passionate Graphics Designer and Frontend Web Developer. I thrive on creating visually appealing and user-friendly digital experiences. With a keen eye for detail and a love for clean code, I transform ideas into reality.

My journey into the world of design and development started with a fascination for how colors, shapes, and interactions can tell a story and solve problems. As a graphics designer, I specialize in branding, illustration, Graphics and UI/UX design. As a frontend developer, I enjoy bringing those designs to life using modern web technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like Bootstrap and React. I also reference the usage of graphics design technologies such as CorelDraw, Microsoft Office, Photoshop, After Effect and Premier Pro

I believe that good design is not just about aesthetics but also about functionality and creating a seamless experience for the end-user. I'm constantly learning and exploring new trends to stay at the forefront of this ever-evolving field.

When I'm not designing or coding, you can find me exploring new creative outlets, learning new skills, or enjoying a good cup of coffee or beverage.
